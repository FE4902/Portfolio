How to Earn

How to Earn

  • 1. Refinery

    You can create MILICO in Gunship Battle: Crypto Conflict at a Lv.18 or Higher Trade Warehouse.

  • 2. How to get MILICO (TITANIUM > MILICO)

    1) You can create MILICO if you own at least 100,000 TITANIUM.

    2) TITANIUM-MILICO Exchange Rate (TIMER) is the exchange rate of MILICO and TITANIUN.
    - Base exchange rate, 1 MILICO : 10,000 TITANIUM.
    - More or less TITANIUM might be required due to the TIMER.

    3) A fee of 10-25% TITANIUM is additionally charged. This fee can be lowered by leveling up the Trade Warehouse.

    4) Created MILICO’s are automatically sent and stored in the WEMIX Wallet that is linked to your game account.

    5) The results of the MILICO production are sent to your mail in game.

    6) Restrictions
    - Only up to 1,500,000 MILICO’s can be produced each day. (Updated every day at 00:00 UTC)
    - Each admiral can only create up to 100 MILICO each day. (Updated every day at 00:00 UTC)

  • 3. How to Exchange (MILICO > TITANIUM)

    1) When exchanging MILICO for TITANIUM, you can receive 0% to 8% additional TITANIUM depending on the level of your Trade Warehouse.

    2) You can only exchange MILICO for TITANIUM if the obtained TITANIUM and the TITANIUM that you already own do not exceed the maximum number of TITANIUM that you can own.

    3) The results of the MILICO exchanges are sent to your mail in game.

  • 4. Connecting the WEMIX Wallet to Your Account

    1) In order to turn TITANIUM into MILICO, a WEMIX Wallet account must be linked your game account.

    2) If the WEMIX Wallet login screen appears during the process of creating or exchanging MILICO, please proceed and connect the account.
