  • STORY

    Protect the world!
    Find global peace!

    In the near future, global warming has accelerated past all projections. With seas on the rise and mass migration across every ocean and continent, conflicts break out across the globe and threaten to become an all-annihilating World War III.

    Armada has released a propaganda video claiming it soon will acquire nuclear weapons of its own. Armada was getting ready to declare a nuclear war, to eradicate peace of the world.

    But, the old world order died in their place, along with countries, military, and your own navy. Only a single battleship, crew, and a deserted island were left behind you.

    In a world full of despair, Will you be able to build the warship to protect the world from Armada, and find global peace?

  • AIRCRAFT CARRIER

    • Kuznetsov

      The Kuznetsov aircraft carrier is designed to launch jets from a short, curved ski-jump ramp. It is capable of carrying a lot of munitions.

    • Elizabeth

      The Elizabeth aircraft carrier has separate bridges for navigation and flight control. It has a range of up to 10,000 nautical miles.

    • Nimitz

      The Nimitz is a nuclear-powered aircraft carrier mass produced to take over similar missions accomplished by Enterprise class aircraft carriers. Large aircraft carrier that can place many jets and AFVs. It has relatively high upgrade cost.

    • Kuznetsov
    • Elizabeth
    • Nimitz

  • FIGHTER AIRCRAFT

    • RF-M

      It is a multi purpose jet that has balanced anti-air, anti-ship, and anti-ground combat abilities. Its lowered speed performance has been fixed by installing canards.

    • EF2000

      The EF2000 is an elite combat jet that is capable of supersonic flights. Its excellent mobility and small radar cross-section make it suitable for gaining air superiority.

    • Desert F-16 FF

      The Desert F-16FF is upgraded version of the F-16FF; equipped with conformal fuel tanks. It also has sensor and radar enhancements. Its operation range and anti-ground attack improved significantly compared to F-16FF.

    • RF-M
    • EF2000
    • Desert F-16 FF

MILICO

MILICO is a interchangeable asset.
You can exchange the in-game currency Titanium into MILICO, and vice versa.

Titanium is a unique resource you can earn through completing daily quest or excavating resource island.
Earn Titanium and upgrade Aircraft, exchange Blueprint, conduct Research and more!
